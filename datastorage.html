Data Storage

This page explains how your Smart Commute data is stored in the system.

What We Store

  • Your login credentials
  • Your class schedule
  • Your starting location
  • Your route and traffic preferences
  • Your notification settings

Where It Is Stored

All data will be stored in a MySQL database and connected through the system’s backend (Java or Python). This ensures your information is saved securely and can be accessed whenever you log in.
